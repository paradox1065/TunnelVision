TunnelVision

AI for Predictive Maintenance

AI is used to predict when infrastructure, like water pipes, will need maintenance before they occur.

Key Features

  • When specific parts of a house or infrastructure are likely to fail, the AI model can alert homeowners or maintenance teams in advance.
  • By analyzing historical data on repairs and failures, the AI can identify patterns and predict future issues.
  • This proactive approach helps in scheduling maintenance during convenient times, minimizing disruption and costs.

What It Does

  • Predicts Remaining Useful Life (RUL) of each asset
  • Sends early failure alerts (next 30 days)
  • Generates a 0–100 risk score
  • Recommends maintenance actions & priorities
  • Visualizes asset health across the city

How it Works

  • 1️⃣ Realistic Synthetic City Data

    • We built a dataset modeled after Cupertino infrastructure using 20+ engineered features:
    • Asset age, material, soil type
    • Rainfall, temperature, traffic
    • Previous failures & repair history
    • Seasonal patterns (ex: storms → storm drain failures)

  • 2️⃣ Machine Learning Models

    • TunnelVision predicts:

    • RUL (regression)
    • Failure probability (classification)

    Models used:

    • Random Forest
    • XGBoost
    • Gradient Boosting

    3️⃣ Risk Score (0–100)

    Weighted by:

    Factor Weight
    RUL 40%
    Failure Probability 60%
    Asset Criticality 20%
    Model Uncertainty 10%

    4️⃣ Recommendations

    The system outputs:

    • Maintenance priority
    • Predicted failure type
    • Urgency level
