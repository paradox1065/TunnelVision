AI for Predictive Maintenance

Project Overview

AI can be used to predict when infrastructure, like water pipes, will need maintenance, reducing costly emergency repairs - more of house maintenance than real estate-related

Key Features

  • When specific parts of a house or infrastructure are likely to fail, the AI model can alert homeowners or maintenance teams in advance.
  • By analyzing historical data on repairs and failures, the AI can identify patterns and predict future issues.
  • This proactive approach helps in scheduling maintenance during convenient times, minimizing disruption and costs.
